Press B1 to begin scanning. The blue LED will turn on.
When the blue LED turns off the devices are connected.
Press B1 to send a speed of 250 rpm.
Press B2 to send a speed of 450 rpm.
Press B3 to send a speed of 1200 rpm.

When the blue LED lights up the motor is STOPPED.
When the green LED lights up the motor is PENDING.
When the red LED lights up the motor is DONE.
